***********************************User Guide****************************************

Welcome to the world of Serial Music!

This software will take you through the composition of serial music (atonal music) 
step by step, from the serialism of melody (generate the note serial), rhythm (using
both Duration Row System and Time Point System to generate the duration for each note)
and volume (generate the velocity for each note).

Please double click the "SerialMusicMaker.jar" to run the program
follow the instructions showed in the Tip Frame and enjoy your journey!
